About Neon Pulse

Neon Pulse is a free reflex-clicking game you can play instantly in your browser, no install required. Click the circles that appear randomly on screen using the correct method before time runs out, and chain combos for an escalating rush of speed and screen-shaking impact.

How to Play

  • Left-click circle — click with the left mouse button (10 pts)
  • Right-click circle — click with the right mouse button (10 pts)
  • Left+Right circle — click both buttons within about 0.2s of each other (25 pts, rare)
  • Hold circle — hold the left mouse button down for about 0.65s (20 pts)
  • Rapid-click circle — click the left mouse button 4 times in quick succession (20 pts)

Circles that time out disappear on their own, resetting your combo and costing you a life. Once your combo passes 6, 14, 24, and 40, the screen's neon-electric effects and sound intensify in stages.

Lives & Progression System

You start each run with 3 lives, losing one every time a circle expires unclicked. Every 20 combo you build earns a life back (up to a maximum of 5), and once your lives run out the run ends and your final score is recorded. Click types also unlock gradually — you start with left-click only, and right-click, left+right, hold, and rapid-click join in one by one as your combo grows.

Multi-language Support

Neon Pulse supports Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, and Spanish. Switch languages any time from the top bar or the start screen.

Who Is This For

Great for anyone who wants to test their focus and reaction speed in a short session, fans of combo-driven arcade games, and anyone looking for a free, lightweight browser game.
